WTYT · Watch The YouTube Things
Step 1 of 3 · What this is

Only watch what's worth your time.

WTYT reads each video and drops a one-glance verdict: watch it, read the gist instead, or skip.

WATCH

Genuinely visual. Worth your eyes.

READ

Informative but not visual. Read the 1-minute gist and reclaim the time.

LISTEN

Music or a performance — just press play and listen.

SKIP

Low value, redundant, or generic filler.

Where it works:

  • Your home feed, scored as you scroll
  • Any video page, scored automatically in the up-next rail
  • Any playlist (including Watch Later) — click the WTYT button to scan it. It only recommends, and never touches your account or plays anything.
Step 2 of 3 · Connect your AI

Bring your own AI key.

WTYT sends each transcript to an AI model to score it. The key stays in this browser and goes straight to the provider. No middle-man server. Pick a provider:

Groq FREE
Free tier, fast. No credit card. Runs GPT-OSS & Llama.
Claude · Anthropic
Sharpest judgment. Paid, pay-as-you-go API.
Start free with Groq — create a key at console.groq.com in about 30 seconds, no credit card. Prefer Claude's sharper judgment? It needs a paid API key (note: a Claude.ai subscription does not include API access).
Advanced — choose model
A good default is picked for you. Change the provider, model, and limits anytime in settings.
Step 3 of 3 · Try it

See it in action.

  1. Open your home feed or any video — scoring starts on its own.
  2. On a busy feed, WTYT scores in batches and pauses; tap Continue to keep going.
  3. For playlists, click the floating WTYT · Analyze playlist button, top-right.
